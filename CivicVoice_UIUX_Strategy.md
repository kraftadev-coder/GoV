CivicVoice: UI/UX Aesthetic Strategy (Editorial Brutalism)

Concept: "The Digital Witness Ledger"

Aesthetic Tone: Refined Brutalist. Heavy on lines, stark contrasts, and prestige typography. It feels like a secret intelligence report meets a grassroots broadsheet.

1. Design Thinking: Context & Commitment

Purpose: Converting "Rumor" into "Primary Evidence" while erasing the human.

Tone: Industrial Editorial. High-contrast black/white/emerald. We use heavy borders (2px+) and monospace data to shout "integrity."

Constraints: Must be ultra-performant on 3G. No heavy blur/transparency on mid-range Androids.

Differentiation (The "Unforgettable" Element): The Emerald Stamp. When a post is verified, a skeuomorphic 3D "Physical Stamp" animation slams onto the post. Users will remember the sound and the visual weight of that verification.

2. Frontend Aesthetic Guidelines

2.1 Typography: "The Voice of Authority"

We abandon generic sans-serifs.

Display (Headings): Fraunces (Soft variant). It’s a variable serif that looks like 1970s print journalism. Characterful, heavy, and undeniable.

Body: Newsreader. Designed for high readability on screens. It feels like reading a prestige leak.

Metadata/Labels: JetBrains Mono. Using a programmer’s font for Geo-stamps signals that this is "Hard Data" that hasn't been tampered with.

2.2 Color & Theme: "Asphalt & Emerald"

Dominant, uncompromising palette.

Primary Surface: #FBFBFA (Raw Bone). Not white. It feels like unbleached paper.

The Ground: #0D1117 (Deep Obsidian). Used for "Security Mode" and Headers.

The Truth Accent: #059669 (Emerald). A sharp, vibrant green used ONLY for verified data.

The Warning: #D97706 (Burnt Amber). Used for "Pending" or "High Risk."

2.3 Motion: "The Staggered Reveal"

Page Load: Items don't fade in. They slide up from the bottom with a 0.05s stagger between cards.

The Amnesia Wipe: When a user submits, a "Static/Noise" texture sweep passes over the screen, visually "erasing" their presence before returning to the feed.

2.4 Spatial Composition: "The Bento Ledger"

Asymmetric Grids: The feed isn't a list of same-sized boxes. "Witness" posts are 2x the size of "Opinion" posts.

Heavy Strokes: Use 2px solid black borders for all cards. No soft shadows. Brutalism relies on structure, not fluff.

Negative Space: Generous gutters (24px) to ensure the "Serious" content has room to breathe.

3. UI Pattern: The "Evidence Frame"

Every photo or video uploaded via the Witness Cam is wrapped in a "Technical Frame":

Top Bar: Displays the browser-detected region (e.g., "LAGOS STATE • DISTRICT 04").

Bottom Bar: Displays the SHA-256 hash of the media in a 10px mono font.

Result: Even a blurry photo looks like an "official piece of evidence."

4. Competitive Differentiation

X/Twitter: Chaotic, rounded, "engagement" focused.

CivicVoice: Structured, sharp-edged, "integrity" focused.

Investors' Buy-in: Investors will see a product that doesn't look like a "social media clone." It looks like an Infrastructure for Truth.
